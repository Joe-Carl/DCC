The Story of Dreamland Christian Center

Dreamland Christian Center was born out of divine instruction, prayer, and unwavering faith in God. What began as a vision placed in the heart of Apostle Solomon Darkwa gradually became a living testimony of obedience and dedication to the call of God.

With a deep burden to see lives transformed and destinies restored, Apostle Solomon Darkwa stepped forward in faith, trusting that God would establish what He had ordained.

Standing faithfully beside him from the very beginning was his wife, Lady Pastor Portia Darkwa. Together, they built not just a church, but a spiritual family grounded in faith, love, and unity.

From small gatherings filled with passionate worship and heartfelt prayers, the ministry began to grow. Lives were touched. Families were restored. Faith was strengthened.

Today, Dreamland Christian Center stands as a beacon of hope, prayer, and transformation under strong spiritual leadership, with Reverend Patrick Ocquaye serving faithfully as the General Overseer.

Dreamland Christian Center is more than a building — it is a divine assignment. It is a place where faith is strengthened, prayers are answered, and destinies are shaped.

The vision remains clear: Changing Lives and Transforming Nations.
